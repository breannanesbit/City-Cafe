Overview

The City Cafe is a little diner on main street of Ephraim Utah. They serve home cooked meals like their famous old fashion meatloaf and Chicken Fried Steak. If you are looking for a fun, good food, family restaurant during your travels The City cafe is a great spot to stop. Run by Owner Whitney Burgess
